2 model categories: for indicator (freq.prob) and truncated frequency.

**For indictor:**
* freq.prob.x: Bernoulli (using Binomial), with x=3, 4, 5, 6 corresponding to 3, 4, 5, 6 latent classes

**For truncated frequency:**
* ztpoisson.x: zero-truncated Poisson, with x=3, 4, 5, 6 corresponding to 3, 4, 5, 6 latent classes
